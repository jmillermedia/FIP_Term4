The Game Is In Your Hands

Introduction to London Referees Group

Who are we?

We are London Referees Group

London Referees Group (LRG) is a nonprofit organization. We are based in London Ontario. We facilitate officials for local hockey games within the region.

What we do:

The third team on the ice.

We serve London and surrounding area for hockey games. Training, developmental camps and officiating programs. Not to mention, we also hold exciting events and leagues.

Why you should be a referee:

Being a referee can be rewarding experience as you can develop a range of skills from personal development to professional development.

You make the calls.

Protect the game.
