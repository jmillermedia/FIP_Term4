About Us

London Referees Group

LRG is a referee association that was established on 15July 2007 at London, Ontario.

We are an With over 225 members association where individual can grow professionally as well as personally. People from all skills level are welcomed at our association.

Vision:

To promote the game of hockey through fair and safe play, and a respect for all individuals involved in the game.

Mission:

LRG is committed to providing a high standard and quality hockeyofficiating in the City of London and surrounding area.

Values:

LRG's core values are integrity, leadership, perseverance, respect and teamwork.
